About Me

I have been a faculty member at Sharif University of Technology since 2003, starting as an Assistant Professor and becoming an Associate Professor in 2011. I am a member of the Advanced Communications Research Institute (ACRI) and have been involved with various research groups at the Iranian Telecommunication Research Center (ITRC). My teaching includes undergraduate and graduate courses such as "Probability and Statistics," "Digital Communications," "Queueing Theory," and "Game Theory."
