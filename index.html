About Me

I have been a faculty member at Sharif University of Technology since 2003, starting as an Assistant Professor and becoming an Associate Professor in 2011. I am a member of the Advanced Communications Research Institute (ACRI) and have been involved with various research groups at the Iranian Telecommunication Research Center (ITRC).

Academic Experiences

  • Assistant Professor at Sharif University of Technology, Department of Electrical Engineering, From 2003 to 2011.

  • Associate Professor at Sharif University of Technology, Department of Electrical Engineering, Since 2011.
