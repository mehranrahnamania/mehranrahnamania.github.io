Biography

Farid Ashtiani (Senior Member, IEEE) received the PhD degree in electrical engineering from the Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2003. From 1995 to 1999, he was partly working with the Power Research Center (P.R.C.) and Niroo Research Institute (N.R.I.) of Iran. From 1999 to 2001, he was a member of research staff with the Advanced Communication Science Research Laboratory, Iran Telecommunication Research Center (I.T.R.C.), Tehran. Since 2003, he has been with the Department of Electrical Engineering, Sharif University of Technology, where he is currently an associate professor and a technical member of Advanced Communications Research Institute (ACRI). His research interests include queueing theory, modeling, analysis, and design of different types of wireless networks, and mobility modeling.
